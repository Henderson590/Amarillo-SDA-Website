Past Events at Amarillo SDA

Fire Deptartment at 2017 VBS!

For our 2017 VBS, the Amarillo Fire Deptartment made an appearance! Getting everyone soaked with the hose, was definitely a VBS to remember. The kids and parents both enjoyed them showing up, having fun with the kids, and giving them a reason to want to come back to VBS.

Free Clinic at the Amarillo SDA Church!

Back in 2019, we hosted a free clinic where people could come and get so many differen things done. Such as, health examinations, dental examinations, eye examinations, etc. all for completely free! It was a HUGE turn out and definitely something we had never seen here at our church before.
