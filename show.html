To Be A Voice

All of us here at the Amarillo SDA Chruch are proud to say that we have our own Sunday Morning Tv Show. To Be A Voice, on the show Pastor Bobby Harris discusses with our guests different topics about the bible. How it correlates to our lives today, and what we can pull from the topic to help us better our understanding of the bible.

To Be A Voice airs every Sunday morning at 6am on News Channel 10. Station 10.1 in Amarillo, Tx.
